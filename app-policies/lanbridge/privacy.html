Network Share Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-30

This policy is aligned with Google Play requirements. Network Share is a LAN-only proxy gateway; traffic is not relayed via our servers.

English (en)

  • Service scope: LAN proxy/forwarding for devices on the same Wi‑Fi/hotspot. No cloud relay. If the host uses a VPN, traffic may traverse that VPN.
  • Data we collect/process:
    • Proxy traffic: relayed locally only; we do not inspect, store, or upload payloads/headers.
    • Local data: configuration, traffic counters, UI state cached on-device.
    • Diagnostics (optional): crash/exception metadata via Firebase Crashlytics (Google). No proxy payloads.
    • Ads (optional): Google AdMob may collect device IDs, coarse location, ad events per Google policies.
  • Permissions (purpose):
    • Network (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE): create local links in the same LAN so devices can communicate. No VPN or remote proxy is created; user data is not sent out.
    • Foreground service / notifications (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, POST_NOTIFICATIONS): show a persistent notification while running so you always know the service status, per Android requirements.
    • Boot completed (optional) (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): only if you turn on “auto resume on boot,” used to restore the last running feature after reboot.
    • Vibration (optional) (VIBRATE): light haptic feedback for operations; no data collection.
    • Not requested: location, contacts, SMS, camera, microphone, photos, external storage, device IDs, or other sensitive permissions.
  • Sharing & processors: only Crashlytics/AdMob when enabled; no selling/renting data. Third-party handling follows their privacy terms.
  • Retention: local cache until you clear or uninstall. Third-party retention per Google policies.
  • Your choices: disable ads/crash reporting where available, clear cache, revoke permissions, or uninstall to stop all processing.
  • Security: traffic stays in LAN; HTTPS is between client and destination. Use trusted networks.
  • Children: not directed to children; no knowing collection from minors.
  • Changes: updates may be announced in-app; continued use = acceptance.
  • Contact: wenyuanshuai@gmail.com
